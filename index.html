iReporter

Change the world in which we live by fighting corruption with us now.

Corruption is a huge bane to Africa’s development. African countries must develop novel and  localised solutions that will curb this menace, hence the birth of iReporter. iReporter enables  any/every citizen to bring any form of corruption to the notice of appropriate authorities and the  general public. Users can also report on things that needs government intervention. Create an account with iReporter or log in to create red-flags or an intervention today.
